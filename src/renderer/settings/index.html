Settings

Screenshot frequency

How often GhostCoach captures and analyzes your screen. No effect on game FPS; more often means fresher reads and a bit more background CPU.

Screenshot quality

High sends a sharper frame to the AI (slightly slower replies, no effect on game FPS).

Riot ID

Name#TAG. Lets your coach use tracker stats (rank, K/D, headshot %) in live tips and chat.

Show tips

When off, tips stay hidden in-game but are still recorded in your history and session archive, so you can review them after the match.

Tip position

Where coaching cards appear on screen.

Tip size

Scales the tip cards in ratio. 100% is the normal size, and the cards stay anchored to your chosen corner.

100%

Pro playbook experimental

The coach pulls proven Radiant and pro habits matched to your exact situation, agent, map, side, phase, and economy, into every tip, chat answer, and match review. Applies instantly.

Frame memory experimental

The coach also sees your previous screenshot, so it understands what just changed instead of judging a single frozen moment. Slightly slower replies, no effect on game FPS.
