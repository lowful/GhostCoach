Settings

Tip frequency

How many tips your coach gives. More tips means more screenshots behind the scenes, and every tip still passes the same quality gates, so more never means worse. No effect on game FPS.

Default
MinimalMax

Riot ID

Name#TAG. Lets your coach use tracker stats (rank, K/D, headshot %) in live tips and chat.

Show tips

When off, tips stay hidden in-game but are still recorded in your history and session archive, so you can review them after the match.

Tip position

Where coaching cards appear on screen.

Tip style

How the coaching cards look. Solid is the easiest to read over a busy screen, Minimal is the least distracting.

Tip background

How see-through the cards are. Lower shows more of the game behind them, higher is easier to read.

90%

Tip size

Scales the tip cards in ratio. 100% is the normal size, and the cards stay anchored to your chosen corner.

100%

Fundamental tips

Curated basics (crosshair, trades, positioning) from GhostCoach's library, mixed in at roughly a quarter to a third of your tips. Great for newer players and Silver and below. Ranked higher? Turn them off for AI-only coaching.

Voice coach

Your coach speaks tips out loud, like an IGL in your ear. Uses your Windows voices, works offline, and never talks over itself.
