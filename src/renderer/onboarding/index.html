Keep it out of your way

The panel should never catch your mouse mid-aim.

While you play, minimize the panel with Ctrl+Shift+M. Coaching keeps running and tips still appear on the overlay. The same shortcut brings it back.

Want the fundamentals mixed in?

Curated basics (crosshair placement, trading, positioning) alongside the AI tips. New, or Silver and below? Keep them on. Ranked higher? Off is the usual pick.

The bar-chart button on the panel opens your Stats: session scores with trends, your recent matches rated 0 to 100, and your weekly report.
