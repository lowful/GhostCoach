Here's the 20-second tour.

  1. 1
    Start coachingSet Valorant to Windowed Fullscreen, then hit the red button on the panel when you're in a match.
  2. 2
    Confirm your agent (important)A bubble asks who you're playing. Tap ✓ or type it. Until you do, you only get general tips, no ability advice.
  3. 3
    Minimize with Ctrl+Shift+MAlways minimize the panel while you play so it can never catch your mouse mid-aim. Tips still show on the overlay.
  4. 4
    Track your progressThe bar-chart button on the panel opens your Stats: session scores with trends, and your recent matches rated 0 to 100.

Coaching keeps running while minimized. Bring the panel back any time with Ctrl+Shift+M.
